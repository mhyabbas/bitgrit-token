Token Overview

Token Ecosystem

GR is built on top of Ethereum’s ERC-20 protocol, the industry standard for smart contracts and issuance of original tokens. This protocol enables us to quantify the value of AI algorithms developed by data scientists on our platform.

Token Significance

By using blockchain technology, bitgrit aims to democratize AI, bring together data scientists from around the world and connect them to companies in need of AI solutions. This collaboration is available to everyone, regardless of experience, resources, or location.

Roadmap

1

2021 Q1

  1. Launch of GR token
  2. Support teams on competition platform to facilitate a collaborative ecosystem
  3. Develop collaboration computation contracts to measure relative contribution of members in development of AI models within a team.
2

2021 Q2

  1. Launch collaboration computation contracts on the competition platform
  2. Introduce a token-centric incentive model for partners
  3. Open a bug bounty and open source contribution program
3

2021 Q3

  1. Introduction of blockchain-backed certification, recognition and rewards
  2. Host events with tokenomic incentives for participation
  3. Introduce custodial token wallets on the bitgrit platform
  4. Test migration of AI competitions to a token-centric platform (PoC)
4

2021 Q4

  1. Complete migration from fiat-based to token-based business model
  2. Integrate our job board with the token ecosystem
5

2022 Q1

  1. Introduction of a token-curated registry (TCR)
  2. Partner with other crypto projects with token interchangeability options
  3. Beta release of the AI Marketplace
6

2022 Q2

  1. Beta release of bitgrit v2.0: A P2P multi-blockchain ecosystem equipped with staking options on the TCR
  2. Full release of the AI Marketplace
7

2022 Q3

  1. Launch developer tools and API communication with the bitgrit mainnet
  2. Additional cryptocurrency pairs for GR introduced

Events

Want to network while learning about high-tech concepts? We organize regular events online and locally in Japan, India, Nigeria, and all around the world! Visit our Meetup page or contact us to learn more and join the discussion.
